✉️ Text blast

      
Sends one text to each selected lead that has a phone. Opted-out / DNC contacts are skipped automatically. Use {{first_name}} to personalize.

      
      
      
⚠ Only text contacts who have consented. This sends now and does not wait for the 8 AM–9 PM quiet-hours window. Reply STOP handling still applies.

      

      

    

  


  
  

    

      
Email blast

      
Sends one branded email to each selected lead with an email address. Unsubscribed and duplicate emails are skipped automatically. Use {{first_name}} or {{name}} to personalize.

      
      
      
      
Email blasts include the workspace signature and unsubscribe footer. Send only to contacts with a valid business purpose.

      

      

    

  


  
  

    

      
Voicemail blast

      
Starts one voicemail drop for each selected lead with a phone number. DNC, duplicate numbers, unknown timezones, and contacts outside local calling hours are skipped automatically.

      
      
      
      
Voicemail drops use the configured recording URL and Telnyx Voice application. A real person answering is hung up automatically instead of playing the recording.

Remove leads a CSV import created today, grouped by source. Your real website / manual leads are never imported sources, so they stay. Removed leads are soft-deleted — find them under Past Clients ▸ Deleted to restore.
